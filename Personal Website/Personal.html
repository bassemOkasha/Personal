MODERN MENTAL HEALTH

Counseling is not “one-size-fits-all.” My approach to individual counseling is deeply rooted in modern mental health philosophy. It’s relevant and relatable. Click below to read more about it.


My approach

I have had success treating even the most resistant clients with my engaging and easy-going therapy style. I don’t believe in forcing therapy if it is not something you enjoy and benefit from.
